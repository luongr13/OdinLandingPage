Making our signature ramen since 1989

Since our opening in 1989, it has been our mission to deliver our best version of Tokutsu ramen to the chicagoland area. Every ingredient is hand selected from the market every morning to ensure quality and our broth is made overnight, simmering for a total of eight hours, to provide a deep and complex flavor.
